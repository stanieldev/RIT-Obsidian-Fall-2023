#Complete 
### Definition
$$dU = T dS - PdV + \mu dN$$
 - $TdS$ : [[Heat\|Entropic energy]]
 - $PdV$ : [[Thermodynamic Work\|Work]], work done by change of volume
 - $\mu dN$ : Energy with gaining particles

### Fundamental Relations ($\mu_i$ = 0)
$$\begin{align}
U &= Q + W  &dU &= T dS - P dV \\
H &= U + PV &dH &= T dS + V dP \\
F &= U - TS &dF &= -SdT - PdV \\
G &= H - TS &dG &= -SdT + V dP
\end{align}$$
